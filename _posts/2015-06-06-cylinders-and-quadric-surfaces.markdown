## Cylinders

```cylinder```

A surface consisting of lines that are all parallel to a given line and pass through a given curve in a plane.

Example: $[ (x,y,z) \in \mathbb{R}^3: x^2 + y^2 = 1 ]$

Example: $[ (x,y,z) \in \mathbb{R}^3: z = x^2]$
