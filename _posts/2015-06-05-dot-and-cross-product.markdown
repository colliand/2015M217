## Dot Product

$$ {\bf{a}} = (a_1, a_2, a_3)$$

$$ {\bf{b}} = (b_1, b_2, b_3)$$

$$ {\bf{a}} \cdot {\bf{b}} = a_1 b_1 + a_2 b_2 + a_3 b_3 $$

## Cross Product

$$ {\bf{a}} \times {\bf{b}}$$
